About Us

HBCU Crowns GSO Outlet was created to bring premium HBCU inspired apparel directly to the Greensboro community. Founded by a CoBE student majoring in International Management with a Sales minor, the outlet was created after identifying an opportunity to bring HBCU Crowns directly to the Greensboro, North Carolina community.

As an official franchise partner of HBCU Crowns, we provide authentic, silk-infused, premium apparel at accessible prices while building a space rooted in community, excellence, and purpose. Our mission goes beyond fashion. We are committed to giving back, uplifting HBCU culture, promoting excellence, and creating opportunities that positively impact the communities we serve.
